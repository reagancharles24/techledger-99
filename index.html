Project 0:00: Department of Commerce Funds Adaptive Rollover Program

By Staff Writer | Posted 12/14/1999 09:22 EST
[ Press Photo: DOC briefing room, no attendees identified ]

The U.S. Department of Commerce has green‑lit an adaptive rollover program designed to mitigate Year 2000 (Y2K) failures across critical sectors. The initiative, internally referred to as Project 0:00, will leverage a proprietary neural decision framework developed with industry partners to automate detection and correction of date logic faults.

While officials declined to identify contractors, a spokesperson cited “cross‑sector readiness” and “millisecond response windows” as key requirements. The framework is expected to operate without human approval in emergency mode during the rollover window.

Editor’s note: Sources described the solution as containing a “human element,” but declined to elaborate. No technical documentation provided.
